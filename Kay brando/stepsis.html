Oro-Va Dental Clinic

Learn More

"The mission of Oro-Va Dental Clinic is to remain a family oriented dental service provider by accessing and meeting customer expectations with regards to affordable dental treatment. Providing proper dental care, and therefore giving tangible value for money beneficial to our clients. Provide all our clients up-to-date information on modern dentistry or solutions."

Oro-Va Dental Clinic was opened in 1980, and is still operational from Santa Ana, Manila up to this date. The founding dentist is Dr. Oliva S. De Luna-Batincela. She established it with a commitment to prevent & treat dental diseases and bring back clients confindence with a perfect smile.

Oro-Va Dental Clinic offers a wide range of services including preventive, restorative, cosmetic dentistry and orthodontics.

To date Oro-Va Dental Clinic has two dental specialists available for specialised dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Focusing on families and working professionals in the urban area seeking comprehensive dental services.
